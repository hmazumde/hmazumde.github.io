Introduction

Terra

Terra will serve as a digital library offering users a space to learn more about herbalism, native plant care, and self-sustainable methods. This resource is for individuals who yearn to feel a renewed connection with their environment and to learn how we can use our vegetative resources to nourish ourselves and the communities we live in. My objective is to encourage individuals (and myself) to implement more ecocentric, healthier practices into our lifestyles.
